⬆️ LendPeak — Self-Serve Conversion Uploads

How we report funded deals to Google ourselves, on our schedule, without waiting on the GHL team. Match key = email (Enhanced Conversions for Leads), so all we ever need from a lead is the email + amount + date.

One-time prerequisite: in Google Ads turn on Enhanced conversions for leads (Goals → Settings → Customer data terms), and the website "Lead" tag must send email/phone (the web team's job). After that, every upload below is self-serve.

The loop (do weekly, or whenever deals fund)

Step 1 — Pull funded deals from GoHighLevel.
  • In GHL, make a Smart List filtered to your Funded stage/status.
  • Export to CSV. You need three things per deal: email, funded date/time, funded amount.
Step 2 — Drop them into the upload sheet. Columns must be exactly this. "Conversion Name" must match the Google Ads conversion action character-for-character (Funded Deal). 
Email          | Conversion Name | Conversion Time              | Conversion Value | Conversion Currency
jane@shop.com  | Funded Deal     | 2026-06-26 15:30:00          | 25000            | USD
mike@cafe.com  | Funded Deal     | 2026-06-27 10:05:00          | 40000            | USD
Set the sheet's timezone parameter to America/New_York (matches the account). For a qualified-but-not-funded signal, use Conversion Name = Qualified Lead instead — same sheet.
Step 3 — Upload to Google Ads.
  • Goals → Conversions → Uploads → +
  • Source: Google Sheets (the connected sheet, auto-imports daily) or Upload a file for a one-off.
  • It matches each row to a click by the hashed email → records a Funded Deal with its value.

Level-up (optional): pull it automatically

Once Leo gives a read-only GoHighLevel API key (or sub-account access), we skip the manual export — a small script pulls Funded opportunities (email + amount + date) and writes the upload sheet for us. Say the word and I'll build that script; until then the manual export above is enough and keeps us fully independent of their team.

Why this beats the GHL-automation route: nothing to build on their side, nothing to break in a workflow we don't control, and we can upload the moment a deal funds. The only thing we rely on Leo's team for is the one-time website tag.
