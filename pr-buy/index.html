Confirming the placements questions for the PR vendor

We verify these before committing spend — they determine whether a placement actually helps the brand search:

  1. Permanent or time-limited? Does the article stay live indefinitely, or get removed after a period?
  2. Do-follow or no-follow links — and how many links / brand mentions are allowed?
  3. Will it index & rank for a "LendPeak" search? Live published URL provided as proof?
  4. Real editorial URL on the main domain, or a separate press-release / sponsored sub-section?
  5. Who writes it — vendor or us? Word count? Can we supply logo / images + contact info?
  6. Labelled "paid / sponsored post"? (affects trust & ranking)
  7. Turnaround time + bulk pricing for the full set, and refund policy if a placement is rejected.
