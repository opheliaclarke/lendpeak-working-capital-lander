Summary — the model in five lines

  • Product: LendPeak purchases a business's future sales at a discount — $50,000 today for $65,000 of tomorrow's revenue.
  • Price: a flat factor rate (1.30), not interest. The $15,000 spread is the disclosed cost.
  • Repayment: automatic, as a percentage of daily sales — it rises and falls with the business.
  • Revenue: the margin (if funded) or a commission (if arranged) — $3,500–$10,000+ net per deal.
  • Our mandate: deliver more qualified merchants at ~$1,500 each, and report which leads funded so the algorithm compounds.
