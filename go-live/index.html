2 · How the tracking works (the "name tag" story)

Think of the gclid as a name tag Google clips onto everyone who clicks your ad.

  1. Person clicks the ad → lands on your page with that name tag in the link.
  2. The page grabs the name tag and saves it onto their lead in GoHighLevel (next to name/phone).
  3. They submit the form → your "Lead" tag fires → Google sees "a lead came in." (this part you're handling)
  4. Days later that lead's deal funds. Someone marks it "Funded" in GHL.
  5. GHL sends that funded lead's name tag + dollar amount to a Google Sheet.
  6. Google Ads reads the Sheet daily, matches the name tag to the original click → "that click made real money."
  7. Google then spends more on clicks like that. That's the whole game.

3 · The GHL tie-in (the tricky part, made simple)

It's just steps 2 and 5 above — two small jobs.

Job A — save the gclid on every lead

Add a hidden field to the pre-qualify form and this snippet on the LP. It grabs the gclid (and the iOS variants + UTMs) from the URL, drops it in the field, and remembers it across form steps:

<input type="hidden" name="gclid" id="gclid">

<script>
(function(){
  var p = new URLSearchParams(location.search);
  ['gclid','gbraid','wbraid','utm_source','utm_campaign'].forEach(function(k){
    var v = p.get(k);
    if(v){ var el=document.getElementById(k); if(el) el.value=v;
           try{ localStorage.setItem('lp_'+k, v); }catch(e){} }
  });
  // if the param dropped on a later step, restore it
  var g=document.getElementById('gclid');
  if(g && !g.value){ try{ g.value=localStorage.getItem('lp_gclid')||''; }catch(e){} }
})();
</script>

In GoHighLevel: create a contact Custom Field named gclid and map the form's hidden field to it, so it saves with each lead.

Job B — send funded deals back to a Google Sheet

In GHL build one Workflow: Trigger = opportunity moved to your "Funded" stage (or status = Won). Action = add a row to a Google Sheet with the fields below.

The Sheet needs exactly these columns (this is Google's offline-import format):

Google Click ID    -> the contact's gclid
Conversion Name    -> Funded Deal
Conversion Time    -> the funded date/time, e.g. 2026-06-25 14:30:00 America/New_York
Conversion Value   -> the funded amount (e.g. 25000)
Conversion Currency-> USD

Then on the account side (my/your part): create a "Funded Deal" conversion action, and in Tools → Conversions → Import → Google Sheets point it at that Sheet on a daily schedule. Done — Google now optimizes to real money.
