Funding is not available in all states. Availability, terms, and required disclosures vary by state. Where required by law — including California, New York, Utah, Virginia, Texas, Georgia, Florida, Louisiana, Maryland, Connecticut, Kansas, Missouri and others — you will receive standardized disclosures (which may include total amount financed, finance charge, estimated APR, payment amounts and frequency, and prepayment terms) before signing.

Eligibility & approval. All funding is subject to approval and underwriting. Stated guidelines do not guarantee approval, amount, or terms. "Same-day" and "4-hour" funding refer to typical timeframes after signing and are not guaranteed. Pre-qualification uses a soft credit inquiry.
