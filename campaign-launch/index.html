6 · Bidding & budget plan

PhaseBiddingTrigger to move on
Days 1–15 (launch / Leo's funded window)Maximize Clicks + $30 max-CPC capGather click + Lead-conversion data without Google starving or overspending. MCA CPCs commonly run $15–$45; the cap blocks runaway auctions.
Once ~15–30 Lead conversions landSwitch to Maximize ConversionsEnough signal for Smart Bidding to optimize to the form-fill.
Once funded-deal (Converted Lead) data buildsTarget CPA on Converted LeadBid to the action that actually makes money — funded deals — not just leads.
$500/day across one campaign. Start Exact + Phrase only (no Broad) — exact for control, phrase for reach. Watch the Search Terms report daily for the first week and feed anything junky into the master negative list.
Budget-focus (this is what sank the plumbing build — learn from it). $500/day in a $15–$45-CPC vertical is only ~15–30 clicks/day. Split evenly across 5 ad groups, none gets enough data to learn and the whole thing stalls. Front-load the 3 highest-intent groups — Apply, MCA, Same-Day — and let Bad-Credit + Big-Ticket run lean until Lead data proves the funnel. Do NOT spread thin and do NOT thrash settings daily; give it a full week before judging. Most importantly: do not enable a single dollar until the Lead conversion is verified firing — untracked spend with no attribution is exactly the failure mode that broke trust before.

7 · Conversion tracking (must be live before enabling)

  • PRIMARY  Lead — form submit on the LP. Bid to this at launch. Enhanced Conversions ON (hashed email/phone + GCLID).
  • SECONDARY  Converted Lead — funded deal, imported offline from GoHighLevel via the captured GCLID. This is the true-ROI action.
  • Install Google Ads tag + GA4 on all /lp/* pages; confirm the form posts to GoHighLevel AND fires the Lead conversion AND passes the GCLID.
  • Verify with Tag Assistant + run the 5 test leads (one per LP, fake 555-01xx phone) before any spend.
