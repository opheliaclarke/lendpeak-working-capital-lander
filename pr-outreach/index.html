2 · Main email (recommended)

Full send-ready emailprofessional · cost-aware · pipeline hook
Hi [Vendor] team,

I manage media placements for a small portfolio of clients in the finance and business space, and I'm lining up our PR partners for the months ahead. Your name came up while researching reputable providers, so I wanted to reach out directly.

In short, we're after guaranteed article placements on credible finance and business publications to strengthen our clients' presence in brand search. Ideally placements that are:

 • Permanent — the article stays live indefinitely
 • Indexed and able to rank for a brand-name search
 • On the publication's main domain (a real article URL, not a hidden press-release section)
 • With at least one brand mention / link (do-follow preferred)

Could you send over:

 1. Your media list — available publications, especially finance & business titles — with per-placement pricing
 2. Any agency, bulk or recurring rates — we place regularly and expect steady volume across several clients
 3. Typical turnaround, and whether content is written by your team or supplied by us
 4. A couple of sample live placements we can review

We're comparing a few partners to find the best long-term value, so a transparent rate card would help us move quickly. To be upfront: this isn't a one-off — we have a number of clients in the pipeline, so we're looking for a partner whose pricing works for ongoing volume rather than premium one-time rates.

Looking forward to your list and your best rates.

4 · Before you hit send

  • Send individually (or BCC) — never CC all 10 vendors on one email.
  • Personalise the [Vendor] line — a 5-second touch that lifts reply rates.
  • Don't name the client (LendPeak) yet — "a financial services brand" is enough until pricing is in.
  • Reply to quotes with the 7 checks from the vendor sheet (permanent? do-follow? indexed? sponsored label? refund?).
