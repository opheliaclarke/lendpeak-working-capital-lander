Important disclosures. LendPeak provides and arranges small-business financing, including merchant cash advances (the purchase of future business receivables), term loans, lines of credit, equipment financing, invoice financing, and SBA loans. A merchant cash advance is not a loan; it is a purchase of a portion of your future sales. Industry funding ranges shown are typical examples, not offers. Products, amounts, rates, terms, and fees vary by product and your business profile and are presented in a written agreement before you sign.

Funding is not available in all states. Availability, terms, and required disclosures vary by state. Where required by law — including California, New York, Utah, Virginia, Texas, Georgia, Florida, Louisiana, Maryland, Connecticut, Kansas, Missouri and others — you will receive standardized disclosures before signing. LendPeak does not provide legal, tax, or financial advice.

Eligibility & approval. All funding is subject to approval and underwriting. Pre-qualification is not a commitment to lend, and final approval is subject to underwriting. Pre-qualification uses a soft credit inquiry.
