Best "make bank" picks cheap wholesale → healthy markup

Cheapest to buy, easiest to resell at 3–5×: IssueWire ($21), Adsy ($25), Stan Ventures ($49), 24-7 ($49), PR Underground ($75), Pressfarm ($90), and PR.com (free–$300). These are pennies wholesale — a client package of a few placements quoted at $250–$600 each leaves a strong margin while still beating any agency's rate card.
Premium / named-site lane: Getfluence and FatJoe are the standouts for real editorial on recognizable finance/business sites — higher cost, but the "as-seen-in" credibility clients pay up for. Use these for the prestige line item.
Vet before reselling: Otter PR and Authority Builders' Digital-PR tier have mixed/critical reviews on quality and billing. Fine to keep on the bench, but test with one small order before you put a client's money (or your reputation) behind them.
