Important disclosures. LendPeak provides and arranges small-business financing, including merchant cash advances (the purchase of future business receivables), term loans, lines of credit, equipment financing, invoice financing, and SBA loans. A merchant cash advance is not a loan; it is a purchase of a portion of your future sales. Products, amounts, rates, factor rates, terms, and fees vary by product and your business profile and are presented in a written agreement before you sign.

Funding is not available in all states. Availability, terms, and required disclosures vary by state. Where required by law — including in California, New York, Utah, Virginia, Texas, Georgia, Florida, Louisiana, Maryland, Connecticut, Kansas, Missouri and other states with commercial financing disclosure laws — you will receive standardized disclosures (which may include total amount financed, finance charge, estimated APR, payment amounts and frequency, and prepayment terms) prior to signing. LendPeak does not provide legal, tax, or financial advice.

Eligibility & approval. All funding is subject to approval and underwriting. Stated guidelines (6+ months in business, $10,000+ monthly revenue, 550+ credit score, active business bank account) do not guarantee approval, amount, or terms. "Same-day" and "4-hour" funding refer to typical timeframes after signing and are not guaranteed. Pre-qualification uses a soft credit inquiry; a full application may involve additional verification.

Contact consent.
